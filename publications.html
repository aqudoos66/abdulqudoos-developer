"Smart Conference Management System (SCMS)"
Abdul Qudoos, Abdul Khaliq, Aijaz Ahmed Arain, Muhammad Furqan, Shahzad Ali. International Journal of Innovations in Science & Technology, Vol. 7, Issue 10, pp. 10-22, November 2025. The Smart Conference Management System (SCMS) is an integrated platform that automates conference website generation, paper submission, reviewer assignment, and communication. It features role-based dashboards, AI-assisted reviews, and automated notifications. Testing showed a 98% reduction in website setup time, 98% notification delivery, and a user satisfaction score of 4.7 out of 5.
